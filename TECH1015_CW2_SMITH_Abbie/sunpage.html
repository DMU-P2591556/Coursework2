This page showcases all of the fun that you can have in the sun. Whenever the weather gets hot, you might be out of ideas on what to do or you want to do to change up your regular sun shine routine. Some ideas could include walks, places such as GoApe, cocktail parties, bikerides, nature trails and outdoor swimming.

This ladybird picture suggests the walks that you can go on if there is a bright sunny day and see what nature walks have to offer that day. Some nature walks in the UK include: Norfolk Coast Path Cotswolds Walk National Trust walks Lake District Wales Coast Path

This video showcases the adventures you can have on the beach. Here we can see a video of birds in the ocean. This is to suggest you to take a beach walk along the coast or pier and enjoy the scenery and wildlfie that you can see. To add another aspect to this you could play a game of eye spy or note how many different animals you see. Some of the different places you can go in the UK are: Brighton Norfolk Blackpool Torquay Anglesey
